Billa Foundation is a non-profit educational organization that aids in the development of schools, students, and instructors.

How We Work

We effect change by bringing together real-world solutions, policy expertise, sustainable finance, and creative collaborations, shaped by decades of experience inside BILLA communities and with its educators.

Finance & Investing

Teachers are change agents in the classroom. So, what happens to student learning when just one in every six teachers in a district receives sufficient training? Adequate financing can assist instructors in developing the abilities required to make a difference.

Technology & Innovation

Using and modifying technology common to the developed world, we deliver goods and services to teachers, parents, and students to improve learning and decision-making.

Partnering with Local Communities

We realize that in order to have a long-term impact, we must actively engage and incorporate the communities we are attempting to assist. We are always learning and improving in our ability to present ourselves as a genuine, ethical partner.
