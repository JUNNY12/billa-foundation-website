Teachers' professional development

About The Project

We assist with the development of the abilities required to make a difference. The paucity of resources accessible to potential instructors, including basic equipment and training, is at the base of the problem. We intend to provide learning materials and to reward teacher training professionals.

Starting point

We discovered the most basic tools for trainee instructors by collaborating with educational specialists. This includes the amount of time needed for training.

Support from local communities

Our aim in Nigeria is to identify the most persistent hurdles to schooling as well as the most critical criteria for early development.

End Result

We were able to provide 48 instructors with the necessary skills to apply in the classroom by listening, engaging, and learning exactly what was required to empower educators in Nigeria.
