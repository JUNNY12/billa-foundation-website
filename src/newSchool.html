Schools' in Nigeria

About The Project

Our initial project was a new school in Nigeria for students under the age of 15. In addition to the long-term effects of the devastating flood in 2010, current schools are critically short in fundamental instructional tools such as stationary, mathematical equipment, and classroom equipment.

Starting point

The first step in comprehending the magnitude of the project was to identify what affected a child's learning experience in Nigeria and how we might enhance.

Support from local communities

The project's location, size, and accessibility were all critical. We intended to have the greatest possible influence on Nigeria's educational scene while being practical.

End Result

Contextualizing the demands of Nigerian pupils, parents, and educators aided us in creating a well-equipped, bright, and accessible learning environment.
